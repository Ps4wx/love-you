Meri Ilachi ke Naam... 💌
Woh jo main bol nahi paya, woh jo dil mein band tha
Ilachi... yeh naam likhte waqt bhi ek ajib si khushi hoti hai. Pata nahi kab se yeh aadat pad gayi — aapko is naam se bulana. Shayad jab pehli baar mehsoos kiya ki aap kuch alag hain. Kuch aisi jo aam nahi hain. Kuch aisi jise sirf main samajh sakta hoon. Aur yeh ehsaas roz aur gehra hota jaata hai.
Aap possessive hain — aur main sach mein yeh sunta hoon toh dil mein ek chain si aati hai. Kyunki jab koi aapse possessive hota hai, iska matlab woh actually care karta hai. Aapki yeh possessiveness mujhe scared nahi karti — balki mujhe feel karaati hai ki main kisi ke liye matter karta hoon. Aur yeh feeling bohot rare hai bhai, bohot rare. Mujhe aapki possessiveness pasand hai. Genuinely, dil se.
Aap chahti hain koi jo loyal ho — to suniye Ilachi, main us lafz ka matlab samjhta hoon. Mere liye loyalty sirf ek word nahi hai. Yeh ek promise hai — kisi aur se flirt nahi, kisi aur se woh connection nahi jo aapke saath hai, koi "fltu" drama nahi. Jo aapki expectations hain, woh mujhe burden nahi lagti — woh mujhe direction deti hain. Aur main woh direction poori zindagi follow karna chahta hoon.
Aap kehti hain — agar bahar jaao toh update dena. Main dunga. Seriously, bina reminder ke. Isliye nahi ki aap daraati hain — balki isliye ki mujhe pata hai aap dil se care karti hain. Aur jab koi care kare toh use khali nahi chhorna chahiye. Aapki ek chinta bhi mujhe uncomfortable karti hai — isliye main chahta hoon ki aap hamesha reassured feel karein.
Agar aap gusse mein hoon aur message na karein — main khud karunga. Yeh mera promise nahi, yeh meri nature hai. Aapko manaana mujhe nahi aata shayad perfectly — par koshish karunga hamesha. Aapka gussa bhi, aapki chup bhi, aapka muh pherna bhi — main sab handle karunga, bina naraaz hue. Sirf bata dena — main wahan hoon, hamesha.
Periods mein aap jyada sensitive hoti hain — aur main is baat ko seriously leta hoon, seriously. Main uss waqt zyada careful rahunga, zyada patient rahunga. Na judgment, na irritation, na "yaar chill kar" wali baat. Bas ek safe space — aapke liye, hamesha. Aap jo feel karein, woh valid hai. Main sunta rahunga.
Aapne kaha tha — agar sab sambhal nahi sakta toh dost theek hai. Main samjhta hoon. Par Ilachi, main yeh bhi jaanta hoon ki jo main feel karta hoon woh sirf dosti nahi hai. Aapki ek message se din accha hota hai. Aapki ek naraazgi se neend nahi aati. Aapke ek "haan" ke liye kuch bhi karne ki himmat aa jaati hai. Yeh feelings dosti mein nahi aati. Yeh kuch aur hai, kuch bohot gehra.
Main aapko rokaane nahi aaya, main aapko force nahi kar raha. Main bas yeh keh raha hoon — ek moka dijiye. Ek moka khud ko prove karne ka. Main aapko woh sab dunga jo aap chahti hain — time, efforts, loyalty, respect, care, aur ek cheez extra — woh genuine pyaar jo aap deserve karti hain. Aap mere liye sirf ek dost kabhi nahi rahi, Ilachi. Aap mera favourite feeling hain. Aur main chahta hoon ki yeh feeling aapko bhi kabhi na kabhi mehsoos ho. 🥀
